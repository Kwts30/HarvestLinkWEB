BRIDGING THE GAP FOR A FRESHER FUTURE

HarvestLink.inc was born from a desire to directly connect farmers in Region 11 (Davao Region) with consumers seeking fresh, local produce. We aim to solve the challenge where middlemen reduce farmer earnings and inflate consumer prices. For example, palay farmgate prices in Region 11 might be around ₱18.50 - ₱21.00 per kilogram, yet consumers can pay ₱49 - ₱55 or more for commercial rice. HarvestLink.inc offers an e-commerce solution to empower our farmers and community, starting in key areas of Region 11 and expanding to Region 12 (SOCCSKSARGEN).

OUR PURPOSE

Our purpose is to revolutionize the way agricultural produce moves from farm to table in the Davao Region and beyond. We aim to build a sustainable and equitable food system that values the hard work of our local farmers and provides consumers with access to the freshest, most nutritious food at fair prices. We believe in fostering a direct link that benefits everyone, creating a stronger, healthier, and more resilient local food economy.
